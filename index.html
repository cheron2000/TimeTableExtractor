About Timetable Extractor

A powerful tool to extract and visualize academic schedules from Excel files.

Features

  • Smart Excel parsing with merged cell support
  • Automatic teacher, division, and room detection
  • Lab session merging (2hr/3hr blocks)
  • Color-coded visual schedules
  • Export and print functionality
  • Session-based caching for fast queries
